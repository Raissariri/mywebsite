Services Provided

La crème de la crème !


There are various ways to use a personal shopper! It all depends on the client needs.

As a personal shopper I might just offer advice to the client during a shopping day.

I can also help if the client doesn't like shopping but needs an outfit for a special event or a full new wardrobe.
Sometimes all the client needs is a little confidence, to get out of their comfort zone, and I am here to help.

My knowledge of fashion and tailoring will provide expert tips and give the highest level of customized advice. Every client is unique and I want to broadcast this uniqueness while shopping for you.

I want to build a trusted relationship with my clients, from sending photos of clothing options, to bringing racks of clothing to their home.

My services are always la creme de la creme, I want you to experience an enhanced shopping experience, without forgetting what the client wants and needs. I will never be pushy with my client, as my goal is to see a smile on their face while looking stylish!

I always maintain the highest level of professionalism in both appearance and attitude. I tailor my work around my client, they can prefer to meet face to face, or facetime, whatever suits you, suits me!

So lets get dressed!
