About Me

"Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak." —Rachel Zoe

Bonjour! It's me, Raïssa, the face behind the shopping!

You might ask yourself, why becoming a personal shopper? It all started with my love of fashion and creativity, I could spend hours looking at fashion magazine, watching fashion shows but what I love most is looking at people street fashion, as for me it is a source of inspiration!

I have travelled all around the World and seen how fashion can be so different from a country to another, even from a city to another.

But what I love the most about fashion, is that there is something for everyone, from a very quirky fashion style like mine, to a very classic look, there is multiple choices.

This isn't just a job to me, it is a passion that I enjoy sharing with every single one of my clients.

xoxo

Raïssa
